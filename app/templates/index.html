Spectral Clustering

About the Application

This application is designed to demonstrate the concept and application of Spectral Clustering, a powerful technique in the field of machine learning and data science. Using a user-friendly interface, you can input parameters and see how they affect the clustering process. The parameters include the sample size, the number of clusters, and the level of noise in the data.

Theory: "On Spectral Clustering: Analysis and an algorithm"

Andrew Ng's paper on spectral clustering provides a deep insight into the mathematical and theoretical foundations of this technique. The paper details the algorithmic process involved, which includes constructing a similarity graph, generating the Laplacian matrix, performing eigen decomposition, and finally applying k-means clustering on the obtained eigen vectors. It highlights the ability of Spectral Clustering to handle non-convex clusters, making it superior to traditional methods like K-means for a wide range of applications.
